Our Heritage

Founded in 2005, Kasavu Kerala has been at the forefront of preserving and promoting the traditional art of Kasavu saree weaving. Our journey began in a small village in Balaramapuram, where a group of passionate weavers came together with a vision to keep this cultural treasure alive.

Today, we are proud to be Kerala's leading Kasavu saree brand, known for our commitment to authenticity, quality, and innovation. Our sarees are worn with pride during Onam celebrations, weddings, and special occasions throughout Kerala and beyond.

20 Years of Excellence

Our Journey

From humble beginnings to becoming Kerala's most trusted Kasavu saree brand.

2005

Kasavu Kerala was founded by Anand Menon in Balaramapuram with just 5 weavers.

2013

Opened our first flagship store in Trivandrum, expanding our reach to customers directly.

2020

Received the State Handicraft Award for excellence in preserving traditional weaving techniques.

2025

Today, we have a community of over 20 artisans and 2 stores across South India, continuing our mission to preserve and promote Kerala's textile heritage.
